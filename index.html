About us

Namma KA18 Coffee Adda has been pouring comfort into every cup since 1991.

We are known for our signature Chikkamagaluru filter coffee, warm hospitality, and the kind of slow mornings that make people return.

☕ The Coffee Culture: Every cup of filter coffee is brewed with patience and passion. From the first aromatic sip at dawn to the evening's leisurely conversations, our coffee connects people, memories, and moments. It's more than a beverage—it's a ritual, a tradition, a bridge between generations.
